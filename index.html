HAOXIN WANG

Research Scientist
Toyota Motor North America R&D - InfoTech Labs

Haoxin Wang is a research scientist at Toyota Motor North America R&D - InfoTech Labs, Mountain View, CA, and is leading the edge computing research project. He received his Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte (UNCC), supervised by Dr. Jiang (Linda) Xie.

His research interests include Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning, Cloud & Edge Computing, AR/VR, and Cooperative Automated Driving.

He is an author of 15+ refereed papers and 15+ U.S. patent applications.

He is serving various roles in multiple academic organizations, including

Guest editor of MDPI Electronics Open Access Journal

Technical program committee member in the Eighth International Conference on Big Data, Small Data, Linked Data and Open Data (ALLDATA 2022) and the Seventeenth International Conference on Digital Telecommunications (ICDT 2022)

Session chair in the Sixth ACM/IEEE Symposium on Edge Computing (SEC 2021)
